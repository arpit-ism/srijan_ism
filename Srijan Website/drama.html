Hallabol

Halla Bol (nukkad natak): ever been part of huge gathering witnessting a commotion on the street? Exploiting this power of a street gathering ,Nukkad natak –the street play competition arouses base human curiosity and keeps alive the romance of street plays . It is time to break the formal barriers and let the message be heard to all .Show your fervour and vigour at Nukkad , the nationwide street play competition .Clear your voices ,get ready for the action ,drum it up folks!Let your message ring out loud.
RULES
1.this is a group event.Each team shall consist of maximum 20 actors ,excluding maximum 2 instrumentalists(optional).
2.The event will take place in a single ground.
3.no props other than dupattas and lathis are allowed.
4.Teams can use enlish or hindi as a language of their play.
5.teams are expected to perform at an open air venue, on a circular stage withaudience at all sides and use of microphones, speakers or music system is not allowed .
6.Time limit for each performance will be 20 minutes.If a team exceeds 20 minutes,it will be disqualified.
7.only live music is allowed.Teams will have to bring their own instruments .
8.the teams are requested to ensure that their presentation is in keeping with the dignity of the fest .Any vulgar or disturbing content of any kind will lead to disqualification.
9.use of fire/water/smoke machines on stage/in close proximity of stage is not permitted.
10.the judge’s descision will be final andand binding.
11.the oraganising team reserves the right to change or modify any of the rules.

Khamoshi

Khamoshi(mime art): Come forward and speak but this time don’t pull up your vocal chords. Time to fire your gestures to burn the world with ideas .Before the human voice was there, gestures served not only to communicate but to aid in the development of vocal sounds. Just like they say; ACTIONS certainly speak louder than WORDS ! MIME requires participation to portray a play , pertain to a theme .
RULES:
• This is a group event and the limit of team size is 15.
• The event will take place in a single round.
• Time limit: maximum 12 minutes and minimum 5 minutes from empty stage to empty stage. negative points will be awarded for not falling within the range of the allocated time .
• Use of any kind of props will lead to disqualification.
• Act containing any kind of vulgarity or disturbing element will be immediately disqualified.
• Decision of the judges will be final and binding.
• The organising team reserves the right to change any of the rules .

Rangmanch

1) The teams are expected to bring their own music on CD or pen drive for the play. Laptop will be provided if needed.
• No recorded vocals are allowed, only instrumental music is allowed.
• All costumes and accessories are to be arranged by the participating team. The organizing committee is responsible for the infrastructural facilities only and shall not be responsible for the security of items left behind in green rooms or on stage after the performance.
• No naked flames, live animals, powder or fluids are allowed on stage.
• Request for special effects and props should be communicated at least a week in advance. The same will be provided only if possible. The request shall be send both by email and confirmed over telephone. Their availability can’t be promised.
• In case of malfunctioning of any equipment provided, the team will be allowed to repeat their performance from the last seen change or from the point of disruption that the team wishes.
• It depends on the judge’s discretion as to how much grace time will be given to the performing team.
• The participating teams are requested to ensure that their presentation is in keeping with the dignity of the fest.
• The judge’s decision will be final and binding.
• The organizing team reserves the right to change or modify any of the rules.
