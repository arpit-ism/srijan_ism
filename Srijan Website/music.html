Unplugged

The event shall be a competition between bands performing self composed or cover songs without aid of electrical music instruments. RULES
1. Each band must have a maximum of three members.
2. Each band will be provided with 20 minutes (including sound check) and points will be deducted for exceeding the time limit.
3. No electric instrument is allowed. (Keyboards can be used in ‘grand piano’ mode only)
4. One set of Drum kit, sufficient no. of microphones and connecting chords will be provided by organizers. JUDGING CRITERIA:
1. Stage presence.
2. Audience response. (Extra points would be awarded for self composition and inclusion of various Indian instruments.)
The decision of the jury shall be final and irrevocable. In case of any confusion about rules the comprehension of rules as stated by the organizers shall be final.

Sursangram

SURSANGRAM: The battle of tunes! Are you the one who gets music ringing inside your head? Are you the one who starts humming the moment you hear music playing ? If yes, then what are you waiting for? Unleash your shackles and show the world what you are capable of in this SURSANGRAM.
EVENT FORMAT:
The event shall have 2 sub-events:
1. Eastern Vocals
2. Western Vocals
Eastern Vocals:
The eastern vocals shall be divided in 2 parts:
1. Prelims, and
2. Finals
RULES:
1. Time limit for prelims is three minutes and five minutes for finals.
2. Orchestra will be provided by the organizers.
3. Participant can have his/her own accompanists (maximum 2 other than participant).
4.Participant can bring his/her own karaoke track(track must not have any vocals mixed with it).
2. Western Vocals:
RULES:
1. Orchestra won’t be provided for western vocals.
2. Participant can bring his/her own karaoke track(track must not have any vocals mixed with it).
3. Participant can have his/her own accompanists (maximum 2 other than participant).
4. Each participant will have five minutes for the round. The decision of the jury shall be final and irrecovable.
In case of any confusion about rules, the comprehension of rules as stated by the organizers shall be the final.
