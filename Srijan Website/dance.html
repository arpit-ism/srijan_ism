Footloose

Footloose

Footloose (Solo dance): The ultimate dance face-off. It is for those who think they can set ablaze the stage and steal the thunder while others stand watching, with awe and jealousy! this is the ultimate quest for greatest dancers .
ROUND 1
1. Contestants will have to perform a part of a prepared dance.
2. Time limit 3+2 minutes.
3. Marks will be awarded for themes, story props etc.
ROUND 2
Two randomly chosen contestants have to perform together on stage on tracks given to them on the spot (30 min will be provided for preparation ). The tracks will be played for one participant at a time for a minute each and then for both the participants for a minute.
JUDGING CRIETRIA
Choreography
Stage utilization
Costumes
Expressions
Adaptability
Synchronization
Spontaneity

Hustle Herd

Hustle herd (group dance): Cut-throat competition over the years has made it one of the most sought- after and popular dance events. Let your steps radiate your ideas and compliment of the music. Stand out in the art of blending the eye, ear and mind. Moreover, this time srijan is pleased to announce Hustle Herd as official HHI audition partner for Jharkhand. Winners would be selected to participate in the Hip Hop India event to be held in Goa and finally they could be off to LA and crowned world hip hop champions. However this benefit is applicable to only for those teams who would follow Hip Hop as the dancing style for the event.
RULES
1. A team must have minimum of 5 active members on the stage.
2. The minimum time limit of the performance will be 7 minutes from empty stage to empty stage.
3. Participants should get their own music/songs on CD and a pen drive. It is advised that they have a back-up on a CD. Team srijan will not be responsible for this.
4. In case of malfunctioning of any equipment provided the team will be allowed to repeat their performance from the beginning or from the point of disruption that the team wishes, with the consent of the judges.
5. Stage specifications: stage area 20ft x 15ft (approx).
6. All costumes, props and accessories have to be arranges by the participating team. Naked flames, hazardous tools & live animals are not allowed.
7. Marks will be awarded for the innovations like theme, story, and props.
8. The music to be used must be submitted to the organisers in a format compatible with windows media player (preferably mp3 format) at least one hour before the event.
9. The teams are requested to ensure that their presentation is in keeping with the dignity of the fest.
10. The decision of the judges and the organising team will be final and binding.
JUDGING CRITERIA:
1. Choreography
2. Coordination
3. Overall impact
4. Expressions
5. Energy level
6. Stage utilisation
7. Costumes

Street Beats

Street Beats: The Here, there is no confined dance floor. Just get up to the music ringing in the atmosphere, rise up and light of the streets. Street beats is one street dancing event where you can prove your mettle and so the hustling bustling crowd what you have got inside.
RULES:
Participants must strictly follow any of the following styles: Hip-Hop, Popping, Toting, Krumping, Break dance, Stepping, B-boying, Contraption and western free style.
Team size – min 4 and max 10
PRELIMS:
1. Time limit – 4-6 min (length of music track)
2. Live music is not allowed
3. All costumes and accessories have to be arranged by the participating team. Naked flames and live animals are not allowed.
4. Participants must get their songs/music on a CD and a pen drive.
5. In case of malfunctioning of any equipment provided, the team will be allowed to repeat their performance from the beginning or from the point of disruption that the team wishes with the consent of the judges.
6. The decision of the judge and the organizing team will be final and binding.
FINAL:
1. At the time of on spot registration, each team will be provided with 3 music tracks (1+1+1 mins) for preparation. Teams are supposed to bring their own portable music player. Different teams would be provided with different music tracks.
2. The teams are to choreograph and prepare to dance to the music tracks provided to them. The total performance duration of each team would be three mins.
3. During the event, every two teams would be grouped together and given a slot. The music tracks of the teams in a slot would all be shuffled and played continuously. The team should be ready and should perform whenever their allotted tracks are played.
4. The one minute of each performance will be considered from empty stage to empty stage i.e. the entry onto and the exit from the stage would be included in the performance.
5. It is not necessary that all the members of the team should perform at all time, but three or more than three participants should be performing at stage on all time.
6. Stage specification: 360 degrees open area.
7. In case of malfunctioning of any equipment provided, the team will be allowed to repeat their performance from the beginning or from the point of disruption that the team wishes with the consent of the judges.
8. All costumes and accessories have to be arranged by the participating team. Naked flames and live animals are not allowed.
9. The participating teams are requested to ensure that their presentation is in keeping with the dignity of the fest.
10. The decision of the judge and the organizing team will be final and binding.
